Financial Risk Management Projects

In my financial risk management portfolio, I implemented two key projects on Value at Risk (VaR). The first project utilizes the Historical Method to calculate VaR, using historical data to estimate potential losses over a specified time period with a given confidence level. The second project demonstrates the Monte Carlo simulation method for VaR calculation, employing random sampling and statistical modeling to assess potential portfolio losses under various scenarios. Both projects provide valuable insights for risk management and decision-making.
